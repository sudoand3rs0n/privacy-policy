Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the FlashCar application collects, uses, and protects user information in compliance with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Spanish law.

1. Data Controller

The data controller of personal information is the owner of the FlashCar application. For any privacy-related inquiries, users can contact the email address provided in the official Google Play app listing.

2. Data We Collect

FlashCar may collect the following information:

  • Email address (when users sign in with Google).
  • Name associated with the Google account.

The app can also be used without an account, although some features will be limited.

3. Authentication

Sign-in is performed through Google authentication. FlashCar does not store passwords or login credentials.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Collected data is used to:

  • Provide user account access.
  • Save test progress and results.
  • Improve the user experience within the app.

5. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing personal data is the user's consent when registering and using the app.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained while the user account remains active. Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

7. Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold or shared with third parties. Authentication services are provided by Google, according to their own privacy policies.

8. International Data Transfers

If any technical services involve international data transfers, these are carried out in accordance with legal guarantees required by European regulations.

9. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request rectification or deletion.
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Object to processing.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, users can contact the email address listed in the official Google Play app listing.

10. Security

FlashCar implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be published on this same page.

12. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users can contact the email address listed in the official Google Play app listing.
